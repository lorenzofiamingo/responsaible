For any restricted transfer of EU personal data to a third country, the firm's
standing position is: confirm whether an adequacy decision covers the importer; if
not, execute the 2021 Standard Contractual Clauses as the Article 46 safeguard and
complete a transfer impact assessment (TIA) before go-live. Treat the SCCs as the
default fallback even where another route appears available, because adequacy and
certification routes can lapse. Never rely on a derogation under Article 49 for a
systematic or repetitive transfer — escalate to the lead privacy partner instead.
