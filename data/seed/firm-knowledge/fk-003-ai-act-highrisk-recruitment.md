In matter 2026-007 we advised that an AI system used to screen or rank job applicants
is high-risk under Annex III(4) of the AI Act, triggering the provider obligations in
Chapter III: risk management, data governance, technical documentation, logging, human
oversight, and a conformity assessment before placing on the EU market. A vendor's
self-declaration that the tool is "decision support only" does NOT remove it from
Annex III where the output materially influences the hiring decision. Document the
intended purpose precisely, because the classification turns on it.
