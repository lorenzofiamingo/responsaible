When a US importer self-certifies under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, the
Commission's adequacy decision permits the transfer without a further safeguard — but
only while the importer's certification is ACTIVE for the relevant data categories.
Before signing, verify the organisation's live status on the official Data Privacy
Framework list and record the date checked in the matter file. Certification is
time-sensitive and can be withdrawn; our practice is to re-check at signing and to keep
the 2021 SCCs ready as a fallback. Onward transfers to sub-processors outside the
framework require their own transfer mechanism.
